clyne

it looks like you are running a fairly old vesion of Mesa, the software OpenGL implementation. Mesa, particularly older versions, is missing key features required by VAPOR. However, it is entirely possible that you have hardware accelerated 3D graphics installed on your system, and that your graphics driver is simply not configured correctly. Most newer systems (less than 3 or 4 years old) have some form of graphics acceleration. They may be integrated into the CPU, or attached via a PCI port. Check with you systems administrator. You may simply need to re-install and configure the OpenGL driver.
